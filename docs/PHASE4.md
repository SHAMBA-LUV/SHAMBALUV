II. The Emotonomic Equation

Value in Emotonomics can be expressed as:

EV = (G × A × I) × R

Where:

EV = Emotonomic Value

G = Gesture Strength (act of giving or attention)

A = Attention Depth (engagement intensity)

I = Impact Breadth (ripple effect of a gesture)

R = Resonance Factor (collective emotional response)

This formula ensures value compounds through participation, not possession.
